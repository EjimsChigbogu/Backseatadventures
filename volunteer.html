VOLUNTEERS WE NEED

Sound & Production Engineers:

You can handle a camera, you take good pictures, you understand sound, lighting and the things that make good production happen. We need you.

Writers:

You can retell stories in a very captivating way, you can outline questions that need to be asked so we can get the full story out of an interview or you understand biographical writing.We need you.

Transcribers:

You can take an audio recording or watch a short documentary/interview and convert it to a written article or a mini-book. We need you.

Project Managers:

You can handle the many details from making contact with a missionary, planning travels, getting the entire team ready, keeping track of checklists, timelines and deliverables? We need you.

Digital Creatives:

You can edit photos, videos, make good graphics or tell stories through creative communications? We need you.

OUR TEAM

David Ogunshola

David Ogunshola was raised as a Missionary Kid in Northern Nigeria and has served as a missionary in Nigeria and Chad with several short term ministry engagements in other countries within Read more West and Central Africa. He grew up around missionaries and his work constantly puts him in contact with many more. He is extremely burdened about the stories of missionaries being preserved for future generations, about the welfare of missionary kids and about the future of missionaries after retirement or when they get old for active field service. He is also a software developer who runs businesses in IT and Education. He lives in Uyo, Nigeria.

Ekene Ugwu

Ekene Ugwu is a missions mobilizer with a passion for christian apologetics, cross-cultural missions and theology. He believes in mobilizing, training and equipping disciples of Read more Christ for the unfinished task of the great commission.
